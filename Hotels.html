Planning a trip to Palawan and looking for the perfect place to stay? Hotels

Four Points Palawan Hotel

Find everything you need to travel the way you like, from a 24/7 fitness center and a large resort pool to restaurants with a sea view and a Kids' Club with indoor and outdoor playgrounds. Begin your day with breakfast at Evolution and end it with inventive Italian cuisine and inspired cocktails at Il Fiore. Step out to exhilarating eco-adventures at Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park and Sabang Mangrove Forest.

Coron Westown

Coron Westown Resort offers guests first class hotel rooms that blend Filipino culture and modern amenities in a total resort experience that is sure to tantalize the senses and relax the body. As the gateway to the islands of northern Palawan, BusuangaAirport is served by the major airlines and is only about an hour from Manila or an hour and a half from Cebu. The resort is just 25 minutes from the airport by shuttle van and just 5 minutes from downtown Coron.

Two Seasons Coron Bayside hotel

Two Seasons enters Coron Town with a bayside hotel that’s truly one of its kind, making vacations in Palawan a lot more delightful in more ways than one. The 48-room Two Seasons Coron Bayside Hotel is Coron Town’s newest top destination for the most breathtaking ocean views and island adventures. Setting a new standard by being the only first-rate accommodation in Coron Town, our hotel offers spacious rooms and suites that spell sheer comfort and undeniable class.

Funny Lion hotel Palawan

If you’re on the hunt for the best hotels in Palawan, be on the lookout for The Funny Lion. The Funny Lion is a pride of well-loved boutique hotels located in stunning locales that offer one-of-a-kind destination experiences. The Funny Lion has dens in Coron, El Nido, and Puerto Princesa (opening in December 2023). While each hotel has its own beautiful design aesthetic, all share a fun and friendly vibe. Always beyond the ordinary.

H Hotel El Nido Palawan

Embrace the ultimate getaway experience at H Hotel, where your every need is meticulously catered for. Bask in the splendor of oceanic or mountainous views from the comfort of your room, perfectly integrated into the natural surroundings with a sense of effortless luxury. Every detail has been carefully curated to ensure your utmost comfort, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the beauty of your surroundings. Prepare to be swept away by the sheer magic and wonder that permeates every nook and cranny of our world-class facilities.

Holiday Suites

In the northern Municipality of San Vicente in Palawan, a hidden destination arises with its beautiful islands and charming community. Holiday Suites Port Barton is the first resort hotel to be established in Port Barton, San Vicente and began its operation in March 2021. Located along the beach of Port Barton Bay, guests are given easy access to the beach and a beautiful view of the sunset.

Seda Lio

Discover a paradise retreat like no other at Seda Lio. Located along Lio Beach, El Nido, Palawan – a prime destination just five minutes from the airport, the 153-room resort-hotel caters to meeting and incentive groups, leisure tourists, families, destination weddings and other discerning visitors.

Astoria Palawan

Astoria Palawan is an eight-hectare resort in the heart of the Philippines’ Final Frontier, Puerto Princesa. The property is the brand’s nod to the natural charm of Palawan. That is why you can expect nothing less than the perfect quiet escape away from the metropolis. Our resort in Puerto Princesa kisses the shores of the majestic Sulu Sea and is a peaceful enclave of emerald virgin forests, grasslands, and mountain ranges. Here is where guests can truly appreciate why Palawan is considered the most beautiful island in the world.

Planning a trip to Palawan and looking for the perfect place to Relax? Resorts

Princesa Garden Island Resort

Princesa Garden Island Resort and Spa offers an exceptional escape located 15-minutes away from Puerto Princesa International Airport. The resort centers on its Palaweño-themed architecture infused with native touches, which blends in with the foliage surrounding the resort. Our event space obtained the prestigious ASEAN MICE Venue Award – making it a reputable space for world class events.

Lihim Resort

Tucked away in El Nido, Palawan, one of the world’s most beautiful islands in the world. This hidden gem offers a serene escape, blending opulence and tranquility. Explore El Nido through our private lounges and beach clubs, indulge in relaxation at Panari Spa, and savor exquisite cuisine with stunning views at Gana and Henri’s. Lihim Resorts offers top-notch Filipino hospitality through personalized butler service.

El Nido coco Resort

Coco Resort emerges as a tropical sanctuary that seamlessly blends natural beauty with luxurious comfort. As guests arrive at the resort, they are greeted by lush greenery, swaying palm trees, and the gentle melody of the ocean, setting the stage for an idyllic retreat.Coco Resort's architecture reflects a harmonious fusion of contemporary design and traditional island aesthetics. Elegant villas and bungalows, adorned with native materials and modern amenities, offer a tranquil escape.

Matinloc Resort

Blessed with beautiful natural sceneries, Matinloc Resort is located in Matinloc Island, Palawan, Phillipines. Matinloc Resort is attracting attention for the best spot for family vacations as well as business meetings. This family-friendly resort takes enjoyment to the fullest with premium guestrooms with ocean view, along with private beach, extensive pools, luxurious massage facility, beach-side restaurant, bar, conference room, gym, and library equipped with not only books and computers but also recreation facilitie Various marine sports to enjoy on the private beach and the resort's exclusive boat to explore the islands of Palio Sea guarantee Matinloc Resort as the best place to enjoy Palawan's attractions.

Cauayan resort

The initial idea was something small and simple, though on a search around El Nido’s beautiful surroundings, they stumbled upon Cauayan and could not resist. Disembarking on the front beach, they met Decen for the first time. Wearing nothing but a pair of briefs and accompanied by his dog Potol, he greeted them as the caretaker of the island -- back then completely wild and impossible to penetrate due to the lush jungle that covered it. Without even knowing who the landowners were, they promised Decen that they will one day own that place, and that he will work for them. Today, both Decen and Potol are still here as part of the Cauayan family.

Daluyon Beach Mountain Resort

Daluyon Beach and Mountain Resort is one of the best hotels in Puerto Princesa because of its secluded location 80 kilometers away from Puerto Princesa City. With the majestic St. Paul Subterranean Mountain Ranges as its backdrop, a Daluyon vacation promises to be relaxing experience with nature.Daluyon Beach and Mountain Resort is a 4-star luxury Puerto Princesa resort with one of the best beaches in Palawan. A model for sustainable tourism, Daluyon has been awarded 3 ASEAN Green Hotel Awards and was given the Hall of Fame Award by the Department of Tourism (DOT) because of this significant tourism achievement.

Astoria Palawan

Astoria Palawan is an eight-hectare resort in the heart of the Philippines’ Final Frontier, Puerto Princesa. The property is the brand’s nod to the natural charm of Palawan. That is why you can expect nothing less than the perfect quiet escape away from the metropolis. Our resort in Puerto Princesa kisses the shores of the majestic Sulu Sea and is a peaceful enclave of emerald virgin forests, grasslands, and mountain ranges. Here is where guests can truly appreciate why Palawan is considered the most beautiful island in the world.

Planning a trip to Palawan and looking for the perfect place to eat? Restaurants

Vellago Restaurant

As the sun sets over the pristine landscapes of Palawan, the Velago Restaurant emerges as a culinary oasis, offering a unique dining experience that harmonizes with the natural beauty that defines the island. Nestled within the tropical haven, Velago Restaurant is a culinary gem that beckons travelers with its promise of exquisite flavors and breathtaking views.The restaurant's ambiance is an extension of Palawan's charm, blending seamlessly with the island's tranquility. Designed to embrace the outdoor allure.

Bella Vita El Nido

Bella Vita El Nido stands as a culinary oasis, offering a diverse and thoughtfully curated menu that seamlessly blends local flavors with international influences. The restaurant is a celebration of Palawan's rich culinary heritage, presenting a gastronomic journey that highlights the region's bounty of fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Whether it's the catch of the day from the surrounding seas or inventive interpretations of classic dishes, every plate is a testament to the culinary expertise that defines Bella Vita.

Piece of Sky El Nido

Piece of Sky El Nido is more than just a restaurant; it's an experience crafted to engage the senses. The interior exudes a casual elegance, featuring tasteful decor and a design that seamlessly integrates with the coastal landscape. Wooden furnishings, earthy tones, and panoramic windows frame captivating views of El Nido's stunning scenery, offering diners a visual feast that complements their culinary journey. The culinary offerings at Piece of Sky reflect a commitment to excellence and innovation. The menu is a testament to the chefs' dedication to utilizing fresh, locally sourced ingredients and delivering a culinary experience that is both artful and flavorful.

Jinjer Restaurant

The Jinjer Restaurant is a traditional-modern concept restaurant with a warm and solemn ambiance. Settled in front of El Nido Bay, it allows you to enjoy the ocean breeze, catch picturesque sunsets, and enjoy stunning views of Cadlao Island and Dilumacad Island. The Jinjer Restaurant serves quality Filipino and international cuisine. We encourage our guests to try our daily special menus, especially “the catch of the day”. Guests love our grilled or baked fish in banana leaves with mango salsa, a must-try when you visit.

YachtWreck Bar

YachtWreck Bar stands as a unique culinary and leisure haven, where the maritime spirit intertwines seamlessly with the breathtaking coastal beauty of its surroundings. As patrons approach this distinctive establishment, they are greeted by an atmosphere that exudes nautical charm, with weathered wood and maritime artifacts creating an ambiance that pays homage to seafaring adventures.The YachtWreck Bar isn't just a dining destination; it's a gathering place for those seeking a maritime escape. The friendly and laid-back staff enhances the welcoming atmosphere, ensuring that visitors not only indulge in culinary delights but also feel a sense of camaraderie in this maritime haven.

TAAS Restaurant

TAAS restaurant is not merely a place to dine; it's a destination that invites patrons to linger and savor the moments. The attentive and friendly staff contributes to the welcoming atmosphere, ensuring that guests feel not only well-cared-for but also embraced by the spirit of Palawan hospitality.

Tomato and Basil Restaurant

The culinary offerings at Tomato and Basil are a testament to the chefs' commitment to authenticity. The menu is a symphony of traditional Italian dishes, expertly crafted using high-quality ingredients and time-honored techniques. From classic pasta dishes infused with rich sauces to wood-fired pizzas adorned with fresh toppings, each item on the menu transports diners to the rustic kitchens of Italy.

Aztec Grounds Bar

The outdoor seating area becomes a lively stage for social interactions, with a tropical breeze and the sounds of upbeat music creating an ambiance reminiscent of a Mexican fiesta. As day turns into night, the bar transforms into a lively hub, where guests can enjoy handcrafted cocktails, share plates of delicious appetizers, and revel in the vibrant atmosphere. As guests step into the establishment, they are greeted by an atmosphere that seamlessly blends the laid-back charm of the island with the lively spirit of a Mexican cantina.
